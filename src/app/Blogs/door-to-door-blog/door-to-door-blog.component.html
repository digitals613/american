How Does Door-To-Door Auto Transport Work?

Door-to-door transport works to pick and deliver your vehicle to your locations. It doesn’t necessarily mean that the transport truck will be parked just outside your house for the pickup and delivery. Mostly due to the narrow and complex location of the customer’s house, you have the freedom to select a place where a transport carrier could be parked and deliver your vehicle.   1. Get A Quote And Book An Order

You can also calculate the cost simply by using an auto transport calculator on the company’s website.   2. Check Up And Schedule The Pickup

    Once you book an order at American Auto Transports for door-to-door transport of your vehicle, our experts arrive at your house. They check and make your car ready for transfer. After that, we schedule a pick-up date for your vehicle. All this process is carried out by the trained professionals of American Auto Transports transport to maximize efficient transport of your vehicle.

  3. We Pick Your Vehicle From Your Doorstep

    On the scheduled date, a transport carrier arrives at your location. This carrier is modified for the door-to-door transport of your vehicle. One important thing to remember is that these trucks will only shift your vehicle, nothing except the vehicle and its parts will be transported. Our professionals, then, load your car onto the truck safely avoiding any damage to the car.

  4. Transportation Of Your Vehicle To Your Location

How Much Time Does Door-To-Door Auto Transport Take?

One of the biggest concerns of customers is when will their transport arrive at their location. Well, as you know our transport carrier departs at the right time when it picks your vehicle and it tries its best to deliver your transport safely and quickly. But there are many factors which can delay your transport. Some of these are as follows:

Distance

As you know, more distance takes more time for the transport carrier to deliver your vehicle to your doorstep. If the distance is 1000 miles or more, it takes on an average of 4-5 days to deliver a transport. While, on the other hand, time taken decreases to about 2-3 days when the required distance is just a few hundred miles.

Size And Weight Of The Vehicle

When you transport a small car that occupies less space and is much lighter, it is quite easy for a carrier to move as fast as it can. In the case of a heavy-weight vehicle, it is quite difficult to control the carrier at high speed, so they make sure not to speed up during heavy vehicle transport. So, the time taken to deliver a vehicle to your destination varies from vehicle to vehicle.

Weather Condition

Your transport can also fall prey to delay due to bad weather conditions. Usually, car shipping companies inform their customers about the delay of their transport during bad weather. It becomes quite difficult and risky for the carriers to move at a steady pace in winter due to snow and bad roads. Bad weather in winter is also the biggest cause of truck accidents.

How Much Does Door-To-Door Auto Transport Cost?

The most basic and important thing that comes to mind after hearing about the door-to-door auto transport is how much it would cost to ship my car. It is quite natural to think about the cost while shipping a vehicle. Cost depends on many factors which include:

Distance Traveled

It is common sense that for the transport over a long distance, more resources of the company are used. So, the cost of transportation also increases with the distance. the more the distance, the more will be the cost for shipping.

Type Of Vehicle

When you transport a lightweight and small car, the car shipping cost will be automatically low. You’ll not need to pay much for that. While, if you want to transport a heavy vehicle you must prepare yourself for the high cost of shipping. The simple logic to this is that it is quite easy for a transport company to transport a small car, unlike a bulky vehicle.

Transport Type

Depending on the type of transport you choose, your cost for shipping varies. Out of enclosed vehicle transport and open vehicle transport, open vehicle transport is cost-friendly while enclosed transport costs much more than open transport.

Season Of The Year

As the difficulty in transportation varies with the weather, so does the cost charged by the transport companies for their services. If you are looking to transport your vehicle in winter, it will cost more than that for summer. It is because, with the increased harshness in weather conditions, transport carriers charge more for their services.

Condition Of The Vehicle

It is significantly easier to transport a fully functional vehicle than a non-operable vehicle. So, if your vehicle is operational, you’ll be charged less than that charged for a damaged vehicle as it requires more effort and safety. For cheap car shipping, you must make your car functional again before transporting it.
