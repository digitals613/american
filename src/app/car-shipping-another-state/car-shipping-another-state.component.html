Choose The Type Of Transport

When you transport a vehicle, there are two types of transport through which you can ship your vehicle. These two are:

Open Vehicle Transport

In this type of transport, your vehicle is transported to your destination through an open carrier on which can carry several vehicles. Open transport is the cheapest way to ship a car to another state. Open carrier has few risks of external factors such as exposure to weather like rain, snow, or hail. If you are looking for an affordable and cheap way of transporting your car, the open vehicle is a great option.

Enclosed Transport

This is a premium way to ship your vehicle to other parts of the country. If your vehicle is expensive, classic, or custom, enclosed transport is the best option as it increases the safety of your vehicle.
