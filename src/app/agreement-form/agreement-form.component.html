American Auto Transports must be notified, should the shipper be unavailable for pick up or delivery, the shipper must have an alternate representative take his/her place as a shipper.

Any claim for loss or damage must be noted and signed on the condition report at the time of delivery.

All carriers contractor will have insurance to cover damage caused by the driver, carrier or carrier’s contractor, weather, act of god, vandalism and or theft during transport. American Auto Transports Solution is not responsible for damage caused by driver, carrier or carrier’s contractor, weather, act of god, vandalism and or theft during transport. All claims must be noted and signed for at the time of delivery and submitted in writing within 15 days of delivery.

Damage must be reported to American Auto Transports Solutions within 24 hours of delivery. Damage must be listed on the BOL and signed by the driver (no exceptions). If there is damage during transport, the client must notate those damages on the final inspection report, pay the remaining balance stated on this agreement, and then contact the carrier’s main office as well as the carrier's insurance company. Failure to notate any damage on the final inspection report releases the carrier of any liability and would result in the inability to process a damage claim. It is customer’s responsibility to review Carrier’s dispatch sheet and confirm the customer correct name and address and verify the identity of the truck driver prior to release the vehicle for transport. American Auto Transports Solution is an acting agent. All claims must be made with the carrier if any circumstances arises. Any/All damages are covered by the carrier’s insurance and must be claimed carrier’s insurance, not American Auto Transports. In the condition of lost or stolen vehicle, all claims must be made with Carrier’s insurance.
