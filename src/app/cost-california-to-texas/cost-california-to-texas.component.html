What Factors Determine the Price of Shipping Your Car from Texas to California?

Several factors includes in determining the cost of moving your car from Texas to California.

The distance between pick-up and drop-off

It is one of the most important elements in determining the cost of your auto transfer. Shipping costs are often calculated per mile and added to the final price.

Delivery mode

Is your vehicle being sent from the terminal to the terminal or door-to-door? This can have a big impact on your total cost. Terminal-to-Terminal transport is often less expensive since it allows carriers to save money.

Route and Seasonality

You can save money if your vehicle can be sent via a popular transport route. Shipping charges are often lower between major cities. The season might also have an impact on pricing. Summer is normally the popular shipping season; thus, rates are higher, but winter is typically the least expensive period to transport your car.

Weight and Dimensions

The weight and dimensions of your car can impact your shipping quote. Larger cars result in greater prices because vehicle transporters have limited room and capacity.
