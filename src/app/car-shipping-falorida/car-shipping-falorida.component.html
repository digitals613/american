Different Ways Of Shipping A Car In Florida

Before hiring the services of a Florida auto transport company for the transport of your car, you must be aware of several ways of shipping available in Florida. These are:

Door To Door

A car transport company picks your vehicle from your doorstep and ships it to your location. This is the most comfortable and easy way of shipping your car through auto transporters in Florida.

Terminal To Terminal

Terminal to terminal is not that much popular as the door to door auto transport, but still, it is an active component of the industry. Some companies will not offer terminal to terminal shipping as there are not many transport carriers that support terminal to terminal shipping.

Terminal To Door

In this mode of shipping, you drop off your car at the terminal and a transport carrier delivers it to your desired location.

Door To Terminal

This type of transport deals with a carrier picking your vehicle up from your doorstep and shipping it to a nearby terminal to your location.
